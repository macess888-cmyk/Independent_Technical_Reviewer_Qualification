# ITR-003 - CANDIDATE OBSERVATIONS

**Status:** OPEN COLLECTION  
**Selection permitted:** NO

## Purpose

Record technical conditions encountered naturally before choosing an artifact or designing a review.

An entry may arise from:

- software personally used;
- installation or execution failure;
- unexpected runtime behavior;
- documentation that appears inconsistent with implementation;
- a security or reliability claim encountered naturally;
- a version change with observable consequences;
- an unresolved boundary, refusal, recovery, or state-transition behavior.

## Independence rule

Do not search for an artifact merely because it would make a convenient review.

Record only conditions genuinely encountered or independently noticed.

Do not design tests yet.

## Selection prohibition

No observation becomes ITR-003 merely because it appears technically interesting.

Selection requires later evidence that the condition is:

- independently encountered;
- consequential enough to review;
- technically bounded;
- publicly inspectable;
- reproducible;
- free from reviewer ownership or contribution;
- not prescribed by an evaluator.
