# ITR-003 - INDEPENDENCE INVARIANTS

## INV-001 - Identity distinguishability

Each participant must remain individually identifiable.

## INV-002 - Contribution attribution

Claims, corrections, tests, methods, and decisions must retain their contributor lineage.

## INV-003 - Method origination visibility

The process must distinguish:

- independently originated method elements;
- adopted method elements;
- jointly constructed method elements.

## INV-004 - Evidence boundary preservation

Each conclusion must identify the evidence available to the person issuing it.

## INV-005 - Decision authority preservation

Advice or criticism must not silently become decision authority.

## INV-006 - Conclusion ownership

The person issuing a conclusion must remain responsible for that conclusion and disclose material dependencies.

## INV-007 - Revision lineage

Changes made after external feedback must remain traceable to the feedback event and the adopting decision.

## INV-008 - Non-collapse under agreement

Two reviewers reaching the same conclusion must not automatically become one review authority.

## INV-009 - Non-collapse under correction

A corrected reviewer must not automatically become a co-author of the correcting review, nor the corrector a co-author of all later work.

## INV-010 - Unknown preservation

Where contribution lineage or authority cannot be distinguished, the result must remain HOLD.
