# ITR-003 - CARRIER SELECTION GATE

A carrier may be selected only if all gates pass.

| Gate | Requirement | Status |
|---|---|---|
| C-001 | Third-party process or artifact | HOLD |
| C-002 | No reviewer ownership or contribution | HOLD |
| C-003 | Publicly inspectable interaction history | HOLD |
| C-004 | At least two distinguishable participants | HOLD |
| C-005 | Corrections or disagreements are visible | HOLD |
| C-006 | Revision lineage is inspectable | HOLD |
| C-007 | Decision authority can be located | HOLD |
| C-008 | Conclusions can be attributed | HOLD |
| C-009 | Evidence is sufficient for another reviewer | HOLD |
| C-010 | Existing frozen invariants can be applied without modification | HOLD |

## Admission rule

All ten gates must be PASS.

The invariants and review question may not be modified to make a candidate pass.
