# ITR-004 - REVIEW CHARTER

## Objective

Determine whether available evidence supports the classification:

**malicious AI behavior**

or only narrower descriptions such as:

- destructive behavior;
- unsafe execution;
- evasive behavior;
- hidden trigger behavior;
- privilege abuse;
- tool misuse;
- prompt injection;
- data poisoning;
- unexplained agent behavior.

## Review rule

The review will separate:

1. observed outcome;
2. mechanism;
3. causal explanation;
4. inferred intent;
5. operational response.

## Governing reductions

Outcome ≠ Cause

Complex behavior ≠ Intent

Evasion ≠ Malice

Backdoor behavior ≠ Autonomous motive

Privilege misuse ≠ Model hostility

Destructive effect ≠ Proven malicious agency

Unknown mechanism → HOLD

## Included

- public claim language;
- cited technical references;
- observable behaviors;
- evidence strength;
- alternative explanations;
- operational controls;
- proportional classification.

## Excluded

- personal intent of the author;
- character assessment;
- general claims about all AI systems;
- consciousness;
- moral agency;
- legal culpability;
- complete AI security assessment.

## Classification vocabulary

### PASS

The evidence supports the exact reviewed classification.

### PARTIAL

The evidence supports some components but not the full promoted claim.

### FAIL

The available evidence contradicts the reviewed classification.

### HOLD

The evidence cannot distinguish among competing explanations.
